Why Start Vaping?

Cigarettes.

We all know the deadly truth behind smoking cigarettes and the ugly result it has on your health. Though, overcoming the addictive nature of smoking is easier said than done. after all, it's the third highest ranked additive substance behind cocaine.

​ Fortunately for people whom smoke cigarettes regularly, the addiction stems from nicotine. which, may I add is not that bad in the grand scheme of things. It's a non cancer-causing nor excessively harmful on it's own. The harm lies within the tobacco and the other numerous chemicals that it's bundled with.

​ Therefore, the individuals whom consumes cigarettes have developed a dependancy on nicotine and hence why vaping is such an amazing alternative to cigarettes. providing an easier escape from smoking, a prolong life expectency and tremendous amounts of health benefits over smoking cigarettes.
​

Health.

​The list below is a very short list of the health benefits:

​ - better oral hygiene
- improved skin health
- better blood circulation
- increased lung capacity
- a greater sense of smell & taste.
- tar build up will begin diminishing
- healthier lungs...

As you can tell there is an unbelievable quantity of reasons not to smoke and there are little to none reason to smoke.
Now, assuming you've decided to quit smoking. We'll now discuss the equipment to what you'll need! ​

Equipment.

The fundamental components:

There are multiple different shapes of a vape. The two most common are VapePen and Mod/tank.

- VapePen: Over the years they've become inexpensive. With better coils with lower ohms capable of providing a better and strong hit.

- Mod and tank: Numerous different designs of mods and tanks exist. These are the most effective way to vape with increased battery life and power.

Designed for both low and high VG e-liquids.

​

How to refill.

This will be a similar experience with most tanks and should not differ immensely.

Mouth to lung:


There is less airflow on the atomiser and drawing will feel identical to smoking a real cigarettes. You first inhale into the mouth and then to the lungs.

Lung Inhale:


Increased airflow and a wider drip tips allowing you to inhale directly into the lungs.

What is PG or Propylene Glycol?


Propylene Glycol is used as a flavouring carrier in food products, as an ingredient in food colouring and also used as an additive in various medicines. PG provides the throat hit sensation and is thinner in consistency than Vegetable Glycerine. Propylene Glycol is non toxic.

What is VG or Vegetable Glycerine?


Vegetable Glycerine is a sweet, vegetable based liquid. VG provides the vapour production which simulates smoke and is thicker than Propylene Glycol. Vegetable Glycerine is non toxic and the more VG in an e-liquid, the smoother it will be to vape.
